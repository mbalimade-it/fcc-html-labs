Yum Yum Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic comfort food that brings layers of rich flavors and hearty textures to the table. With its perfect combination of tender pasta sheets, savory meat or vegetable filling, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted golden cheese on top, this dish is both satisfying and delicious. Whether you're cooking for family, friends, or just treating yourself, this lasagna recipe is simple to follow and promises a mouthwatering result that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 200g shredded mozzarella
  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • Salt, pepper, and dried herbs to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, heat a little olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in the canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer some meat sauce, then lasagna sheets, then ricotta, and repeat until all ingredients are used. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and sprinkle mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake for 25–30 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it rest for 5–10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
